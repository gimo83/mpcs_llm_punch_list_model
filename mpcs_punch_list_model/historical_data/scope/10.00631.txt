1.0 PROJECT OVERVIEW

The Contractor shall provide all labor, materials, equipment, and supervision required for the complete engineering, procurement, construction, installation, testing, and commissioning of the Process Interface Building PIB-19 (PMCC-05) . This is a process interface facility designed to house telecommunications equipment, server racks, UPS/battery systems, and associated support infrastructure.

The project scope includes all required disciplines: Civil/Structural, Safety Systems, Fire Detection & Alarm, HVAC with blast/fire dampers, Electrical, IT/Telecom power, and all access/egress requirements per Saudi Aramco Engineering Standards (SAES).
